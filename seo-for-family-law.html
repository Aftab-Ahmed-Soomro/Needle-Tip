User-Friendly Website Development

An attorney website needs to be user-friendly at most. Its primary purpose is to connect with customers and make them feel comfortable. Our development team works on every aspect of a law firm's website.

Competitor Analysis

Our team always goes through a detailed analysis of our client’s competitors and relatable market. It helps us to understand if the project is capable enough to compete with other law agencies. SEO Technical Audit

Many attorneys are unaware of the difference SEO technical audits can bring to a website. By going through this strategy, we remove the indexing of webpages that add zero value to a website. A family law platform must provide critical and necessary information only. It is not a website to entertain customers or keep them engaged unnecessarily.

 

Only serious customers looking for family lawyers visit such websites. Keep in mind, a law firm website must be aware of legal issues too. For instance, having two different versions of your website available at once can cause trouble for the owner. Often a website is available with its https and non-https versions together on the internet.

 

It can confuse customers and leak any client’s potential information too. Thus, we make sure that such technical issues do not hinder our client’s website. It is our responsibility to make sure that every website is free of errors, spam, and technical issues.

 

 

Local Search Availability

According to reports, more businesses are moving towards local SEO. It is the best way to make an attorney's website available in local search results. When people around you are searching for the services that you provide, local SEO makes you their priority in SERP results.

 

At Needle Tip, we consider local search visibility as a crucial aspect of business growth. Our clients can attain half of their customers from nearby locations. Thus, our experts can optimize your attorney website for local searches by following a few steps.

  •     Claim your Google My Business profile.
  •     Add links to your website from legal and local directories to boost your online presence.
  •     Update the address and geo-specific location of your family law agency.
  •     Create location-specific content with customized keywords and meta titles.

We try our best to increase the availability of a project website in service areas. The more cases a family lawyer can handle, the better experience he/she can earn.
