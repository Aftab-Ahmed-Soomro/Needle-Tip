How Stable Is iOS 18 Beta? Complete Details Here

iOS

By John Fernandes    modified Apr 11, 2025

~ 3 minutes to read

    In our smartphone driven world, Apple is considered as one of the most popular and recognizable brands. The company’s contribution towards the smartphone industry has made it iOS users favorite. Since its launch of the iPhone, people keep waiting for another iOS update to experience technology from the future.  According to statistics, the overall revenue of Apple was estimated to be more than 383.2 billion U.S. dollars in 2023.  This number is going to take a significant rise this year with the launch of iOS 18 Beta. 

    This iteration of the iOS 18 was released a few days back on Aug 12, 2024.  From tech enthusiasts and developers to business professionals and everyday users, individuals are more than excited about the fourth public beta version of iOS 18. However, the new features and improvements of every beta version bring a question of stability for the users.

    Is the IOS 18 beta stable for everyday use? What type of bugs and fixtures come along with this iteration?  Read this article and delve deeper into the details of the iOS 18 beta to resolve all your queries.  But before we proceed, let us understand what the beta version is.

    What Is iOS 18 Beta?

    The beta version of any operating system can be defined as a pre-release version that is launched in the market for testing purposes.  It is made available for a specific set of users including early adopters and developers who can try out the new features of this iteration and provide healthy feedback to the company.

    These reviews help Apple to release a seamless and stable OS with its final version for the public.  Most often, beta versions are considered less stable than the official iteration. These include different bugs and performance issues, unlike the original iteration.

    New Features in iOS 18

    There are several ios 18 new features and improvements implemented through which users will come across a promising experience. A few of the most prominent features are discussed below.

    Enhanced AI Capabilities

    The fourth public beta version of iOS 18 offers deeper integration of AI all over the system. Users will experience smarter Siri suggestions and improved text production along with a more customized experience tailored according to user preferences.

    Interactive Widgets

    The widgets of iOS 18 are redesigned to appear more dynamic. Besides sharing information at a glance, these include different interactive elements through which users can easily perform actions directly through their home screens.

    Revamped Notifications

    The notifications for this iteration of iOS 18 are redesigned to manage and organize everything easily. These notifications will also enable users to remove clutter and remind users to focus on necessary apps and tasks.

    Privacy Enhancements

    Apple has made sure to prioritize privacy in iOS 18 by implementing features like more transparent and detailed app tracking along with improved permission management.

    Improved Multitasking

    The latest version of iOS enables fluid multitasking through which users can perform plenty of tasks simultaneously without any lags. This feature is especially beneficial for iPad users.

    Stability of iOS 18 Beta

    General Performance

    The most important feature to consider in the beta version of an operating system is general performance. In the case of iOS 18,  the reviews show mixed feedback in this area. According to individuals using the iPhone 14 and iPhone 15 or any other newer devices, the experience was smooth. However, users of older models like the iPhone 12 or earlier encountered occasional shutdowns.

    While most users experienced smooth animations and transitions, some also encountered lag when trying to run multiple apps.  Also, users who tried to operate resource-intensive apps like video editors or games were unable to enjoy a smoother experience.

    Battery Life

    Another important consideration of the beta phase is the battery life of the OS.  According to user feedback, the battery performance of the iOS 18 beta is unpredictable. A few of the users have reported faster battery drainage as compared to iOS 17, especially when exploring new features running multiple apps simultaneously.  

    However, according to the previous beta releases by Apple Incorporated, the battery performance is noticed. Indicates that the company has been focusing on optimizing power consumption.

    App Compatibility

    The next point to consider when testing beta versions is the compatibility of apps. Because developers require ample time to upgrade their apps and make them completely compatible with the latest version of the OS, a few apps may not work smoothly during the beta phase.  When talking particularly about the iOS 18, plenty of market-leading apps are running without any errors.

    Nevertheless, some third-party apps that may particularly rely on complex APIs have encountered unexpected shutdowns or crashes.  Individuals who often use banking apps, business-related apps, or any other critical apps can avoid installing the beta version on their primary devices.

    Bugs and Glitches

    As mentioned earlier most beta versions of an operating system consist of bugs and glitches. The same is the case with iOS 18 beta. The most discussed bugs and rich according to reports are as follows.

    Wi-Fi And Bluetooth Connectivity Issues

    According to some users, the operating system made it difficult to maintain a stable Wi-Fi connection or perform Bluetooth pairing with other devices.

    App Crashes

    Different apps that were not updated according to the requirements of iOS 18 crashed unexpectedly.

    User Interface Glitches

    Users have reported minor glitches in the user interface Such as overlapping of text or misalignment of app icons. Remember that, these are considered cosmetic errors that do not impact the functionality of the operating system.

    Audio Issues

    Individuals who were using wireless headphones particularly encountered audio lags or lower sound quality. These issues are often adapted by the earlier versions of iOS and may be removed in the original version. However, they can be problematic for early adopters.

    Security And Privacy

    No matter which version of Apple, the company has always prioritized its security standards. Users have stated that the iOS 18 beta practices the latest security protocols hmm and focuses on user privacy. Nevertheless, because it is a beta version we are talking about, users must be cautious about storing sensitive data as a precaution.

    Is iOS 18 Beta Worth Installing?

    If you are still considering the question of whether or not to install the iOS 18 beta version, try to focus primarily on your usage.

    iOS 18 Beta for Developers

    Developers must prefer installing the beta version to check the compatibility of their apps with the original iteration. Stability is the main requirement for testing your apps. Also, the beta version will help you have an idea about the latest features according to which apps must be updated.

    iOS 18 Beta for  Enthusiasts

    Tech geeks who enjoy exploring new features or feel excited about resolving occasional bugs or errors can install the beta version for an adventure. However, it is recommended for such users to install the beta version on a secondary device through which they do not lose or risk any sensitive information.

    iOS 18 Beta for Everyday Users

    If you are a smartphone user who requires your device to function properly for everyday tasks,  installing the beta version is not a good idea. As it is a pre-release iteration of the iOS, users must not risk their apps or important data and wait for the official version to be released.

    Final Thoughts

    The IOS 18 beta according to users seems a promising update with plenty of improvements and exciting features.  However, similar to any beta version, it has its flaws that will be fixed after the original release.  To install the beta version or not is an individual's choice that may totally depend on his or her usage.

    If you are tolerant of bugs and looking to explore new features before the release, this is the best time to try this iteration.  Because Apple has launched several updates to get over the flaws and glitches, it will surely release a promising version of iOS 18. Until then, keep exploring and enjoying the iOS 18 beta version to experience the new possibilities driven by Apple.
