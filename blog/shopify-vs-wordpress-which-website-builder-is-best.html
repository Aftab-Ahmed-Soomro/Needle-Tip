Shopify Vs WordPress: Which Website Builder Is Best In 2022?

Web Development

By John Fernandes    on May 10, 2022

~ 3 minutes to read

    Today’s digital era is popular for website building and online business.  It is why entrepreneurs are giving attention to the building and maintenance of their websites. Experts always recommend choosing the best website builder to compete in the digital market. To help you, we have picked the two most popular website builders on the Internet – Shopify, and WordPress.


    Both platforms offer ample features for the development and maintenance of any website. However, you may find it difficult to pick one. Therefore, the easiest way to make a choice is through the evaluation of both platforms. Below, we have discussed some particular details about both website builders. If you wish to build your online brand and make a user-friendly web design, keep reading.


    Shopify vs WordPress: How to Pick the Right Website Builder?


    Remember, the first step to building your website is to know the niche of your business. For instance, your business may revolve around sale and purchase, blogging, newscasting, podcasting, or any other sector. Each of these builders offers a specific set of features. To help you, we have compared the most recommended ones.


    A Platform for Drop Shipping


    Often retailers give attention to drop shipping services for securing their physical products. Also, it is the best way to keep from warehousing costs. Therefore, business owners consider having this option on their websites.


    Fortunately, both platforms Shopify and WordPress include print-on-demand services. Also, these let you connect to warehousing services easily. As a result, you can send your orders effortlessly to your buyers all over the globe.


    A Platform for Search Engine Optimization


    SEO is one of the most important factors for promoting your business online. Interestingly, Shopify, as well as WordPress, offer tools for Search Engine Optimization. Hence, you can edit your XML sitemap or robots.txt file anytime.


    Also, you can optimize the Meta title and Meta description of your website. All you need to do is to invest in the paid subscription of Shopify or WordPress development services. In the case of Shopify, these tools are built-in. However, you will feel the need to download the free Yoast SEO plugin for WordPress.


    A Platform for Mobile-Friendliness


    In today's era of smartphones, having a mobile-friendly website is mandatory. Picking Shopify development services for website building is beneficial in this case. It is because all of its templates are responsive and automatically adjust according to the device.


    WordPress, in contrast, has variable templates. Henceforth, one always needs to check the compatibility of every template with the device. For more, you can give attention to Accelerated Mobile Pages (AMP). By using these, you can boost the page load speed.


    A Platform for Advantages


    Both website builders have their pros and cons. Experts consider WordPress for its limitless customization. Among the lows and highs of wordpress.org is its need for coding skills. Also, it requires you to pick a domain name, web host, and security provider on your own.


    When using Shopify, technical knowledge does not matter. Also, you can get the web host and security in its subscription. However, it does not perform like user-friendly drag and drop software.


    A Platform for Features


    The different features or both website builders make it easier to pick one. By using WordPress, you can quickly add a blog or an e-store to one of your blogs. Also, through the drop shipping service, you can sell whatever products you like. This platform also lets you host online forums and effortlessly build a website whenever you like.


    Moving on, Shopify comes with a user guide, which helps to build an online store in QuickTime. Also, this platform includes building tools for digital marketing. Because it is an e-commerce platform, it offers a set of product management and inventory tools too. When using this builder, you can retail across major networks like eBay and Amazon.


    Shopify Vs WordPress: Which Platform To Choose?


    The biggest difference between WordPress and Shopify is versatility and type. Shopify is a limited-use platform specially used for the creation of online shopping stores. WordPress, on the other hand, helps create anything from personal blogs to affiliate websites.


    Moreover, Shopify is a self-hosted website builder. WordPress is a combination of two versions, including wordpress.com andwordpress.org.


    When one needs to launch their personal blog, wordpress.com is the right pic. The other version of WordPress is self-hosted software that can create almost any website on the Internet.


     Therefore, we have compared Shopify with wordpress.org. Now, selecting your website builder majorly depends on your purpose behind the website. Consider either platform based on the above-mentioned aspects. If the standards of your brand fall in the line of the website builder, then it is the one!
