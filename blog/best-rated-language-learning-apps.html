Top 10 Best-Rated Language Learning Apps in 2024: Your Ultimate Guide

Mobile App Development

By John Fernandes    modified Sep 15, 2025

~ 4 minutes to read

    Hola! Ciao! Bonjour! Marhaba! Konnichi Wa! Assalam O Alaikum & Hello – Different languages with the same greeting! It is what our modern world is about now! Globalizing in a way that helps us experience different languages and cultures. 

    According to recent research, there are approximately 7139 different languages all over the world. Learning a few popular ones among these can be more than just a hobby for many people. It can serve as a gateway to multiple career opportunities, new cultures, and better corporate connections. Interestingly, with the ongoing advancements around us, you can easily find a variety of language-learning apps all over the web.

    These platforms cater to all the needs and levels of people for engaging in language learning because of the reason.  Rather than investing more time in visiting learning centers or finding a nearby institute, simply download these language learning applications with beginner-friendly features, multiple languages, and a flexible price range. If you have heard about this idea for the first time, here is a detailed guide to help you come across the best 10 language learning apps of 2024.

    The Best Language Learning Apps Of 2024

    1. Duolingo

    Why It Is Used?

    Duolingo is our first choice as a language learning app because it provides entertaining and short lessons to help people learn different languages. It offers a game-like structure that includes various achievements, rewards, and streaks through which users find it fun to learn a language casually.

    Primary Features

    •     Consist of more than 40 languages
    •     Teaches popular languages like German, Spanish, and French.
    •     Classes are also available for unique languages like Navajo and Hawaiian.
    •     5-minute lessons that focus on listening, writing, vocabulary, and grammar.
    •     Features story tracking through which users are motivated and engaged.
    •     Active discussion forums to help users interact and learn.

    Pros And Cons

    •     Offers a free version with complete access to multiple languages.

    •     Unique gaming-like structure for easy learning.
    •     Cross-platform app that can operate on iOS, Android, and desktops.
    •     Multiple ads in the free version can annoy users.
    •     Does not offer deep-level learning for advanced users.

    How Much Does It Cost?

    Besides the free version, it offers a monthly subscription fee of $6.99 through which users can enjoy and add a free version with offline access.

    Who Is It For?

    Duolingo is considered a perfect language learning app for beginners and casual learners who wish to experience new cultures and languages.  It is the best one for those who want short and quick lessons for learning on the go.

    Available Platforms: Android, iOS

    2. Rosetta Stone

    Why It Is Used?

    Rosetta Stone is a great language-learning app known for its immersive language-learning approach. The platform teaches our languages to users without any translation. Its full immersion technique is considered as a way to teach people a new language by making them feel as if it is their target language.

    Primary Features

    •     Consist of more than 24 languages.
    •     Offers a speech recognition technology to help users improve their pronunciation.
    •     Offline access is available through which users can learn a lesson anywhere and anytime.
    •     Real-world immersion technique to teach users a language without translation.

    Pros And Cons

    •     Full immersion method encourages users to think in a new language.
    •     Speech recognition technology enhances pronunciation.
    •     Offline lessons can be easily downloaded on the device.
    •     Higher subscription fees as compared to other apps.
    •     A slow learning pace can annoy users. 

    How Much Does It Cost?

    It offers a monthly subscription fee of 11.99 dollar or users can pay a lifetime subscription of $179.

    Who Is It For?

    Rosetta Stone is a comprehensive language learning tool for serious learners and professionals who wish to learn a new language by mastering its basics.  Also, it makes a great choice for individuals who prefer the immersive learning method rather than translation-based learning.

    Available Platforms: Android, iOS

    3. Babbel

    Why It Is Used?

    Babbel is used as our traditional language learning platform that focuses on improving real-life conversational skills. It consists of structured lessons that stand out from the market's traditional and academic approach to practical learning rather than simple memorization.

    Primary Features

    •     Consist of 14 different languages like Russian, Spanish, Italian, and French.
    •     Offers short 10 to 15-minute lessons that focus on the primary areas of language.
    •     Prioritizes in teaching conversation, grammar, and cultural context.
    •     Offers speech recognition to improve pronunciation.
    •     Personalized reviews and progress tracking to help first-time users.

    Pros And Cons

    •     Highly structured lessons that help to learn a language from basic to advanced.
    •     Teaches through real-life conversations, which makes it a great choice for expatriates and travelers.
    •     Offers an ad-free environment.
    •     Does not provide a free version due to which casual learners may find it difficult to use.
    •     Limited language is available when compared to competitors.

    How Much Does It Cost?

    It offers a monthly subscription of $12.95. However, long-time subscribers may be offered discounts.

    Who Is It For?

    Babbel can be considered as an academic language learning app for people looking to learn a language above the basic level. Also, it Is a great tool for professionals and travelers who wish to learn communicating in a new language.

    Available Platforms: Android, iOS

    4. Busuu

    Why Is It Used?

    Busuu is an AI-based language learning app that offers interaction-based lessons by giving users the chance to practice with native speakers. It consists of different language exercises that are checked by language experts and native speakers to help people learn and speak in a social environment.

    Primary Features

    •     Offer 12 different languages, while majorly focusing on European languages.
    •     Allows users to personalize their learning plan as per their professions.
    •     Consists of an offline mode for learning anywhere and anytime.
    •     Exercises are checked by native speakers along with expert tips.

    Pros And Cons

    •     Provides personalized learning and progress tracking.
    •     Real-time feedback from native speakers.
    •     The free version is available with basic features.
    •     For native speakers, the facility is only available in the paid version.
    •     Not a good choice for non-European languages.

    How Much Does It Cost?

    It offers a free version with core features along with a paid version with a monthly subscription fee of $9.99.

    Who Is It For?

    Busuu makes a social language learning tool for individuals who wish to practice a new language with native speakers.  It makes the best choice for people who are looking to learn European languages.

    Available Platforms: Android, iOS

    5. Memrise

    Why Is It Used?

    Memrise, as the name implies is a language learning that focuses on a space-to-repetition system to enhance the user’s memory retention. It helps people to quickly learn in different languages by providing audio and video lessons from native speakers. Users find it a great choice as it helps to improve listening and pronunciation.

    Primary Features

    •     Offer 16 different languages along with regional dialects.
    •     Community-driven courses to help users learn countless language lessons.
    •     Uses a spaced repetition technique that helps to improve vocabulary.
    •     Video and audio lessons help users improve their pronunciation and listening skills.

    Pros And Cons

    •     Help to improve vocabulary.
    •     Beginner-friendly lessons focused on memory retention.
    •     User-generated lessons to encourage community contribution.
    •     Does not focus on grammar or conversation skills.
    •     Some users find the lessons repetitive.

    How Much Does It Cost?

    It offers a free version for casual learners. However, users can enjoy its paid version with a monthly subscription fee of $8.99.

    Who Is It For?

    Memrise makes the best language learning tool for individuals who wish to focus on proving their vocabulary the new language. It is a helpful choice for visuals and for the three learners who are traveling or looking to learn basic phrases quickly.

    Available Platforms: Android, iOS

    6. Pimsleur

    Why Is It Used?

    Pimsleur is an audio-based language learning tool that primarily focuses on speaking and listening in a new language. It helps people on the go by offering short or do-you lessons that people can listen to while commuting or performing their everyday tasks.

    Primary Features

    •     Offers more than 51 languages.
    •     That teaches less commonly taught languages like Urdu and Swahili.
    •     Audio-based lessons to help improve speaking and listening.
    •     Encourages offline learning through downloadable lessons.
    •     Helps to practice conversation from the start.

    Pros And Cons

    •     Enhances speaking and listening in a new language.
    •     Flexible lessons that encourage multitasking.
    •     Offers a practical and conversational environment for learning.
    •     Lack of writing and reading exercises.
    •     Expensive as compared to competitors.
    •     Does not offer a free version.

    How Much Does It Cost?

    It is available add a monthly subscription fee of $14.99.

    Who Is It For?

    Pimsleur is the best choice for auditory listeners and busy professionals who wish to learn a new language while multitasking.  It is what will for the travelers who want to improve their conversational skills.

    Available Platforms: Android, iOS

    7. Mondly

    Why Is It Used?

    Mondly is an advanced language learning app that uses the latest technologies like virtual reality and augmented reality. The app is a fun platform as it immerses users in an interactive learning environment while giving a touch of future-based approaches.

    Primary Features

    •     Offer 33 different languages, which include regional languages.
    •     Encourages immersive learning in an AR and VR-based environment.
    •     Improves conversation by offering AI-based chatbots.
    •     Detailed learning lessons to improve grammar.

    Pros And Cons

    •     AR and VR features encourage users to practice learning.
    •     Quick learning lessons with a focus on grammar.
    •     Visually attractive and interactive lessons.
    •     The free version does not provide VR and AR features.
    •     Chatbots are not advanced enough to have complex conversations. 

    How Much Does It Cost?

    It offers a free version for casual learners but people can download its premium and paid version with a monthly subscription fee of $9.99.

    Who Is It For?

    Mondly is a modern language learning tool for tech enthusiasts who wish to learn a new language by engaging in a visually attractive environment.  It is often preferred by basic learners or intermediate learners who fish too quickly to have a grip on new languages.

    Available Platforms: Android, iOS

    8. Drops

    Why Is It Used?

    Drops is a helpful language learning tool that stands out in the market for its quick 5-minute visual lessons that focus entirely on vocabulary. It is a great choice for learners who find it difficult to dedicate their time to learning multiple languages.

    Primary Features

    •     Offer more than 45 languages.
    •     It teaches natural languages like Maori and Icelandic.
    •     5-minute quick lessons that encourage vocabulary learning.
    •     Simple and attractive design to keep users engaged.
    •     Focuses on memorization and dissociation.

    Pros And Cons

    •     Visually appealing lessons to encourage learning.
    •     Quick lessons that provide fast-paced learning.
    •     Offers an extensive number of languages to learn.
    •     Does not improve conversation or grammar.
    •     The free version does not allow more than 5 minutes of learning every day.

    How Much Does It Cost?

    It offers a free version with five-minute visual lessons every day. However, users can download its premium and paid version with a monthly subscription fee of $9.99.

    Who Is It For?

    Drops make the best choice for casual and visual learners who wish to learn a new language quickly. Also, it is preferred for individuals who are looking to learn short phrases and sentences in a new language.

    Available Platforms: Android, iOS

    9. HelloTalk

    Why Is It Used?

    HelloTalk is more like a social media platform for language learning that helps people learn different languages by communicating with native speakers from all over the globe. It allows users to communicate through voice notes or messages with different language experts which can help to improve fluency in a new language.

    Primary Features

    •     Offer more than 150 languages. 
    •     Allows communicating with native speakers through text, audio, and video.
    •     The chat interface consists of translator and grammar correction features.
    •     Built-in quizzes and games for language learning.

    Pros And Cons

    •     Encourages practicing with native speakers.
    •     Free version available with access to basic features.
    •     Built-in tools to rectify grammatical errors.
    •     Does not offer structured lessons for learning.
    •     Some native speakers may not interact according to the user's expectations.

    How Much Does It Cost?

    It offers a free version with basic features but users looking for the paid version can pay a monthly subscription fee of $6.99.

    Who Is It For?

    HelloTalk is basically created to help social learners who are looking to practice their speaking and listening skills with native speakers.  It makes the most of a conversational approach for people who are looking to practice a new language in their community.

    Available Platforms: Android, iOS

    10. Fluenz

    Why Is It Used?

    Fluenz, the last language learning app on this list offers a structured type of learning with lessons that are dependent on each other. It follows a more classroom-like method of teaching different languages by offering a traditional and detailed class. Each of its lessons is focused on a clear explanation of culture, syntax, and grammar.

    Primary Features

    •     Offer 7 different languages including Mandarin, Spanish, and French.
    •     Teaches through detailed video tutorials.
    •     Extreme focus on vocabulary and grammar.
    •     Offline access to videos is available for learning anywhere and anytime. 

    Pros And Cons

    •     Detailed explanations and structured learning patterns make it ideal for serious learners.
    •     HD quality video tutorials for user engagement.
    •     Offline access is available for the app.
    •     Expensive price tag as compared to competitors.
    •     Limited languages are available as compared to other apps.
    •     The free version is not available for casual learners.

    How Much Does It Cost?

    The app does not have a free version and offers a paid version with a monthly subscription fee of $18.

    Who Is It For?

    Fluenz is created for serious learners, students, and professionals looking to learn a new language while focusing on syntax and grammar. It offers a detailed learning method which makes it ideal for individuals looking for structured lessons.

    Available Platforms: Android, iOS

    Why Language Learning Apps Are So Popular in 2025?

    Language learning apps have become a popular trend nowadays for their outstanding affordability, accessibility, and compelling learning methods.  These platforms serve as a helpful tool for everyone who is looking to update their lifestyle by learning new cultures and languages.  

    With the rise of cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence, augmented reality,  and virtual reality, these apps are providing flexible solutions for everyone who is looking to learn key phrases in different languages or wishes to practice communicating in different communities.

    Mondly is the best example of such a language-learning app that can easily transform the way one learns multiple languages. Besides, apps like Drops and Duolingo that use a game-based approach to learn different languages make the overall process fun rather than difficult.  

    Furthermore, apps like HelloTalk and Lingoda based on real-world conversations offer a way through which you can easily practice with native speakers and boost your confidence level and conversational skills. With the launch of such apps in the market, learning new languages has become easier for everyone.

    Choosing the Right Language Learning App for You

    To learn a new language, it is necessary to choose the right language-learning app. Make sure to focus on your personal goals, learning style, and daily routine. You must know if you are looking to interact with a native speaker or if a more traditional approach will help you out and learn the desired language.  Ask yourself if you are interested in a structured grammar-based learning experience or if you wish to experience speaking a new language casually.

    As of 2025,  there are a myriad of options out there for users who wish to learn new languages. Whether you are looking to travel to a new country and wish to explore it, or you want to learn a new language for business communication purposes.
    Get ready to immerse yourself in a new culture easily through one of the language learning apps discussed above. Explore a new dialect and syntax in the comfort of your home. Also, share your experience with others and motivate them to learn new languages too.
