What is a Platform AI Model? Everything You Need to Know

Artificial Intelligence

By Ramsha Irfan    on Nov 18, 2024

~ 3 minutes to read

    Artificial intelligence, most probably known as AI, has been serving as the primary source to revolutionize industries all over the world.  A prominent step forward to this is platform AI.  More clearly, platform AI models can be described as flexible and scalable platforms for different applications. According to Grand View Research, the AI industry is most expected to grow at a CAGR of 37.3% and reach approximately 1.8 trillion U.S. dollars by the end of 2030.  It will become possible due to the heavy adoption of models throughout the industry.

    As they perform better than traditional specific models, business owners prioritize AI models to streamline operations, drive innovation, and boost customer experience.  Moreover, a report from McKinsey explains that companies prioritizing platform AI models have experienced a 30% increase in productivity what do you do for faster decision-making and automation? Let us dive deeper into the details of platform AI models and explore everything about them.

    Understanding Platform AI Models

    An Artificially Intelligent platform or a platform AI model can be easily understood as and pre-trained system that can be easily customized to enhance task performance across multiple applications. Rather than creating different AI models for every task, developers are more likely to create platform AI models with flexibility and scalability. Businesses leverage this as they can use a single perform multiple tasks like Predictive Analysis, Image Recognition, Natural Language Processing, and much more.

    Professionals looking to improve the AI capabilities of their ventures find it as a cost-effective approach. Moreover, these models are based on different elements like Deep Learning frameworks and Machine Learning algorithms. Due to these foundational elements, these models are highly adaptable and perform seamlessly through multiple industries and sectors.

    Key Features of Platform AI Models

    Scalability

    Platform AI models can easily handle a high quantity of user data and work seamlessly under heavy loads. This scalability makes them capable of growing through the organization and working across multiple industries because data continues to grow endlessly.

    Interoperability

    These AI models allow integration with multiple software systems and databases.  It is a great way to exchange data seamlessly while working across multiple departments within our company. This way, professionals can utilize data from multiple resources and generate a better analytics report.

    Pre-trained capabilities

    Some of the platform AI models are trained by going through large data sets through which companies can easily utilize them while skipping their training phase. It can be considered a built-in feature that is beneficial for faster deployment and limits the use of extra resources before implementation.

    • Customizability

    Business owners can easily customize these platform AI models as per their particular requirements by adjusting their settings, behaviors, and responses.  This type of personalization makes AI models perfect for platforms with unique goals.

    • Multimodal Capabilities

    Advanced level models can also process and respond to different types of inputs including any form of media like text, images, and audio. Due to this multimodal capability, these models can perform multiple tasks like sentiment analysis, image captioning, or any kind of complex decision-making.

    Types of AI Models in Platform AI

    AI models can be easily categorized on the basis of their design and functionality.  Organizations can benefit from all these types of platform AI models and attain multiple benefits through a single platform.

    1.    Natural Language Processing (NLP) Models

    NLP Models are capable enough to interpret and generate human language. These are commonly used in chatbots and virtual assistants. Also, these models can assist during sentiment analysis and language translation. Interestingly, Open AI's GPT 4 and Google's BERT utilize these models for operating as customer service chatbots, text analysis tools, and powerful virtual assistants.

    2.    Computer Vision Model

    Computer Vision Models help during tasks like image and video analysis. They are capable enough of identifying patterns in visual data, which explains their vast use in areas like autonomous driving, medical imaging, and facial recognition.  The best examples of these models are Amazon Rekognition and Google’s Vision AI. These are popularly recognized for their major roles in healthcare, security, and retail.

    3.    Speech Recognition Models

    Speech Recognition Models, as their name implies, are used to interpret audio data through which applications like transcription services, call center analysis and voice-activated assistance operate seamlessly.  Undoubtedly, the most popular examples of such models include Apple Siri, Amazon Alexa, Google Speech to Text.  These platforms widely assist humans during real-time transcription and hands-free interaction.

    4.    Predictive Analytics Models

    Predictive analytics models are extensively used to identify risks, forecast trends, and improve the power of decision-making. They do so by analyzing historical data through which they can generate detailed insights and assist professionals all over the industry. For this reason, these types of platform AI models play major roles in sectors like healthcare, logistics, and finance. The two most popular examples of these models are IBM’s SPSS and SAS Advanced Analytics.

    5.    Recommendation Models

    Recommendation models, as per their name, play a key role in popular sectors like streaming, advertising, and e-commerce. These models can help to suggest relevant products and services by prioritizing user preferences. They analyze content based on user data to help individuals in these industries. The two most popular examples of this type of model are Amazon’s Product Suggestion Algorithm and Netflix’s Recommendation Engine.

    6.    Generative AI Models

    Generative AI Models are the finest type used to generate new content in the form of music, images, or text because of their training based on different data patterns.  These types of models are majorly used across multiple sectors like personalized marketing, content creation, and other creative fields. The two most popular examples of these models include DALL-E for image generation and ChatGPT for text generation.

    7.    Reinforcement Learning Models

    Reinforcement learning models are more like learners rather than providers. They evolve by receiving feedback from users or any other resources from their environment through which they become ideal for optimization tasks or dynamic decision-making. These models are probably used for complex problem-solving.  The two most popular examples of these models are AlphaGo and DeepMind’s AI from the gaming and robotics sectors which help us to understand the process of reinforcement learning.

    8.    Multi-modal AI Models

    Multimodal models, as discussed earlier are the ones utilized for processing and combining various data types in the form of audio, images, and text. They are popularly used in inexpensive applications like content generation, autonomous systems, and customer service. The two prime examples of these models are OpenAI's CLIP and GPT 4.

    Benefits of Using Platform AI Models

    The most prominent benefits of using platform AI models are discussed further.

    • Cost Efficiency

    Platform AI models can be pre-trained through which professionals can customize them for various tasks.  It is a great benefit for organizations as they can cut their finances to develop basic models from scratch.

    • Faster Time-to-Market

    Platform AI models can help to reduce development time through which they can enable faster deployment of AI-powered projects and solutions.  It is considered a plus point in marketing sectors due to extreme competition because speed and innovation help businesses stay at the forefront.

    • Improved Decision-Making

    Platform AI models Play a major role in processing and analyzing real-time data to generate actionable insights. It helps business owners to make informed decisions quickly.

    • Enhanced Customer Experience

    Platform AI models based on Natural Language Processing can provide personalized responses and instant replies to customer queries. This way, clients can get instant replies without experiencing long waiting times. It helps to boost customer experience and enhance satisfaction rate. 

    • Scalability and Flexibility

    Platform AI models also include features like flexibility, adaptability, and scalability. These benefit businesses by easily improving their AI capabilities and proceeding to successful growth without investing in extra costs of required infrastructure changes.

    Role of Platform AI Models in Different Industries

    These artificially intelligent platform models play major roles across multiple industries. Have a look at a few of these.

    • Healthcare

    In the healthcare sector, these models are extensively used to help professionals with patient data analysis, personalized treatment recommendations, and detailed diagnostics. For example, Watson Health by IBM makes the most of artificial intelligence to support clinical decisions by interpreting the medical data of patients.

    • Finance

    In the finance sector, NLP Models and Predictive Analytics Models can assist professionals in customer service automation, risk assessment, and fraud detection. For instance, platform AI enhances the operation of JP Morgan for compliance and transaction monitoring.

    • Retail and E-commerce

    In the retail and e-commerce sector, AI-based recommendation engines play a major role by providing customers with a personalized shopping experience, which helps to boost sales. Most probably, Alibaba and Amazon have been using platform AI models to optimize inventory and increase customer engagement rates.

    • Manufacturing and Logistics

    In the manufacturing and logistics sectors, these models can help to predict maintenance needs, streamline logistics, and optimize supply chains.  For instance, AI-based models are being used by General Electric (GE) to monitor equipment performance which can minimize downtime and prevent operational costs.

    • Media and Entertainment

    In the media and entertainment sectors, these models assist in personalized marketing, content recommendations, and generating creative content. Its benefit can be understood by understanding the performance of Netflix’s recommendation engine as it helps to keep viewers engaged.

    • Automotive

    In the automotive industry, platform AI models play a major role by offering predictive maintenance, driver assistance, and autonomous driving. As per reports, Waymo and Tesla make the most of these models to improve navigation, self-driving capabilities, and vehicle safety.

    Real-World Examples of Platform AI Models

    These artificially intelligent platform models are also used in real-world applications. A few popular examples are mentioned further.

    1.    OpenAI’s GPT-4

    GPT 4 is an advanced-level multimodal AI platform that can easily process images and text. It is used to create content and provide customer support across multiple industries. Also, it is widely used in different educational apps.

    2.    Google Cloud AI Platform

    The cloud AI platform by Google Helps to provide comprehensive learning and natural language processing tools across different industry sectors.  Its performance is recognized in major areas like healthcare, retail, and logistics.  Also, it assists in natural language understanding and demand forecasting apps.

    3.    Amazon SageMaker

    Amazon SageMaker is a type of AI platform model that is used to process and support the development, deployment, and training of different machine learning models.  It is serving multiple industries in the form of recommendation engines or through apps used for product personalization and fraud detection.

    4.    IBM Watson

    IBM Watson is a popular AI model recognized for its capabilities of Predictive Analysis and Natural Language Processing. It is recognized in the healthcare sector by helping medical professionals improve diagnostics, support treatment planning, and interpret patient data.

    5.    Meta’s LLaMA (Large Language Model Meta AI)

    LLaMA is a foundational AI-based language model that is extensively used by researchers for the development of advanced-level NLP applications. This process is mostly performed and research sectors are focused on academics and enterprises.

    Future Scope of Platform AI Models

    When looking forward to a futuristic approach of platform AI models, major improvements can be seen, and features like Continuous Learning, Machine Learning, and Ethical AI. With expected developments in self-learning capabilities, these platform models will be able to evolve without the need for any retraining.  Experts believe these AI-based systems will become autonomous.

    Also, some prominent concerns about transparency and ethics of these models may become the reason behind further improvement.  Developers are making sure to make these models accountable and ethically accurate. When talking about the industry-specific solutions of these models, the future looks promising with their roles majorly enhancing the finance, legal, and healthcare sectors.

    Finally,  these AI models are the most expected to provide a better collaboration between human experts and AI. It will lead to better decision-making and shape the modern world in a better way.

    FAQs

    1.    What is the difference between platform AI models and traditional AI models?

    Platform AI models are versatile and adaptable. They can easily handle multiple tasks across various domains. Traditional AI models, on the other hand, are particularly created to handle one task at a time.

    2.    Are platform AI models secure?

    Yes, most of the platform models provided by Amazon and Google consist of advanced-level security measures.  Users can trust these for their secure APIs and data encryption algorithms.

    3.    Are platform AI models beneficial for small businesses?

    Platform AI models can benefit small business owners because of their scalability and customizability.  These models can help entrepreneurs to improve their customer service and decision-making.  Moreover, companies with limited resources can utilize these models for streamlining their operations.

    4.    Which industries can majorly benefit from platform AI models?

    Platform AI models can majorly benefit industries like health care, e-commerce, finance, and logistics.  These models can be used for everything from automation and decision-making to predictive analysis and customer engagement.

    The Takeaway

    According to a report by Deloitte, approximately 86% of executives report that AI models have become key tools in their organizations as they enable enterprise scalability. These models are undoubtedly transforming the way industries operate different tasks.  As technology continues to evolve, the potential of these AI-based models will most likely expand and make them an integral tool for multiple industries.
