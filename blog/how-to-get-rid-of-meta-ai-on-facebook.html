How To Get Rid of Meta AI on Facebook – A Detailed Guide

Metaverse

By John Fernandes    on Jul 24, 2024

~ 2 minutes to read

    The world where we live is more like an ever-evolving sphere of cutting-edge technologies where social media plays a vital role. According to a DataReportal, there were approximately 5.07 billion social media users all over the world. This number was estimated in the month of April this year.  When evaluating the reports, particularly for Facebook, the social media channel was reported to have 3 billion monthly active users.The most important reason for Facebook to be used extensively by more than 2.09 billion users every day is its continuous advancement.  Currently known as Meta,  the social media platform is completely aimed at providing users with an outstanding experience through the latest features. One such feature of Facebook is the Meta AI, which is more like an artificially intelligent feature that is specially designed to boost user interaction and recommendation.

    Unfortunately, not everyone is fascinated by these features or has the desire to use them. In fact, plenty of users on Facebook look around to minimize or remove the presence of Meta AI for a personalized experience.  Because more than 4.8 billion people are using smartphones in 2024, Facebook is accessed by individuals on iPhones and androids.  

    Looking forward to helping all of these,  we have shared a detailed guide for users looking forward to disabling Meta AI on Facebook.

    Introducing Meta AI on Facebook

    Facebook has embedded Meta AI with multiple aspects of its platform for a user-friendly experience.  Users can find it everywhere on Facebook while exploring friends' suggestions, content recommendations, chatbots, and targeted advertisements. While many users consider these AI features as an ideal tool to boost experience in the form of customized content, others are concerned about invasion of privacy and control over their social media accounts.

    It is why many users are looking forward to disabling or limiting their Meta AI experience for multiple reasons. The three primary reasons for which users are looking forward to disabling Meta AI are, 

    1. Better organic interaction
    2. Data privacy concern
    3. Traditional Facebook experience

    How to turn off ask Meta AI on Facebook?

    Adjust privacy settings

    One of the most prominent ways through which meta-AI controls a user's Facebook experience is by collecting different forms of data.  For this reason, users can limit Facebook regarding their data collection by adjusting their privacy settings.  Focus on the steps discussed below to remove Meta AI on iPhone and android.

    For iPhone:

    1.    Launch the Facebook app.
    2.    On the bottom right corner of the app, click on the three horizontal lines to open the drop-down menu.
    3.    From the drop-down menu, scroll down and click on the option settings & privacy.
    4.    Now, click on the option settings.
    5.    Now, explore the privacy section and search for options like privacy shortcuts, privacy checkup, and your Facebook information.
    6.    Turn off the location history and adjust your data permission settings to quickly limit the collection of data for different websites and apps.

    For Android:

    1.    Launch the Facebook app.
    2.    On the top right corner of the app, click on the three horizontal lines to open the drop-down menu.
    3.    From the drop-down menu, scroll down and click on the option settings & privacy.
    4.    Now, click on the option settings.
    5.    Now, explore the privacy section and search for options like privacy shortcuts, privacy checkup, and your Facebook information.
    6.    Adjust the settings for the location history and collection of data on different apps and websites.

    Manage Ad Preferences

    Meta AI please a major rule to optimize the ads that users see on Facebook.  By adjusting the ad preferences of this social journal users can help to eliminate AI-based ad targeting. Managing ad settings on Facebook offers p settings for iPhone and android users. 

    For iPhone And Android:

    1.    Launch the Facebook app. 
    2.    Find the option settings & privacy. 
    3.    Click on the option settings to open the dropdown menu. 
    4.    Scroll down to the ads section.
    5.    Click on the option ad preferences.
    6.    In the ad settings section, adjust your data collection preferences from target ads and partners. 
    7.    Review the section advertisers and businesses.
    8.    Eliminate any advertisers you don’t want to impress my clients.

    Limit Personalized Content

    Meta AI plays a major role in personalizing the content available on your screen.  Users can easily find content of their interest. However, users can follow a few steps to make sure they experience more organic content.

    On iPhone:

    1.    Launch the Facebook app.
    2.    On the bottom right corner of the app, click on the three horizontal lines to open the drop-down menu.
    3.    From the drop-down menu, scroll down and click on the option settings & privacy.
    4.    Click on the settings option. 
    5.    In the news feed settings section, click on the option news feed preferences. 
    6.    Adjust as preferences to the option prioritize who to see first.
    7.    Click on the option unfollow people to hide their posts, and reconnect with people you unfollowed. 

    On android:

    1.    Launch the Facebook app.
    2.    On the top right corner of the app, click on the three horizontal lines to open the drop-down menu.
    3.    From the drop-down menu, scroll down and click on the option settings & privacy.
    4.    Now, click on the option settings.
    5.    Go to the section news feed settings and click on the option news feed preferences. 
    6.    Adjust your preferences to customize your news feed by following a few people and unfollowing others to create a more personalized and manual experience.

    Disable Facebook Activity Tracking

    Most people also look for how to turn off Meta AI on Facebook on iPhone and android as the social media channel tracks user activity all over the web boosting the influence of Meta AI on an individual's account.  By disabling this feature,  users can easily limit the control of Meta AI.

    For iPhone And Android:

    1.    Launch the Facebook app.
    2.    Find the option settings & privacy. 
    3.    Click on the option settings. 
    4.    From the dropdown menu, navigate to the option your Facebook information.
    5.    Now, click on the option off-Facebook activity. 
    6.    Click on the option manage your off-Facebook activity. 
    7.    Clear all your history and turn off the future activity of Facebook stop this show media app from tracking user activity outside of Facebook.

    Remove AI-Driven Chatbots

    For those who are frequent users of businesses and other pages that own artificially intelligent chatbots, there are ways to prevent  AI from intruding.  Both iOS and android users can follow these steps to get over the issue.

    For iPhone And Android:

    1.    Launch the Facebook app. 
    2.    Navigate to the messages option to launch messenger. 
    3.    Search for the conversation that you want to remove with the chatbot. 
    4.    Click on the conversation to open it. 
    5.    Click on the name mentioned at the top of the chat and move to the section chat settings.
    6.    Click on the option block or remove to eliminate any future interaction with the chatbot.

    Alternative Measures to Consider

    The Use of Different Third-Party Apps

    The internet offers a variety of third-party apps start browser extensions people are responsible for reducing AI interaction and tracking on the Facebook platform.  Unfortunately, these are not completely reliable resources and offer their own set of risks.  Users are advised to go through detailed research about such third-party solutions to make sure they are safe.  Experts recommend looking for user reviews and the reputation of the third-party solution on the market before making any decision.

    Staying Updated with The Latest Policies

    One of the easiest ways to limit AI on Facebook is to stay informed every time.  The social media channel keeps updating its features and policies according to the latest trends. Users who are completely aware of the latest privacy features or settings can easily maintain the influence of artificial intelligence on their user experience.  Individuals are advised to keep reviewing their Facebook settings and go through the necessary adjustments.

    The Impact of Reducing Meta AI

    Individuals who keep searching for how to turn off Meta AI on Facebook on android and iPhone must be aware of the changes in their experience after limiting the influence of this feature.  Below are the proper energy changes that you may experience once you reduce or eliminate Meta AI from your social media account.

    • Reduced Customized Content

    Your news feed will most likely feature general content rather than sharing customized content that is particularly tailored to user interest.

    • Less Targeted Ads

    The featuring advertisements on your news feed will be less likely to relate what your preferences.  Users will come across general advertisements, however, these will give a less intrusive vibe to many.

    • Better Privacy

    When you reduce the data collection and tracking of Facebook on your smartphone, it will quickly enhance your privacy settings.  Hence, users will come across a secure social media experience.

    • Higher User Control

    Users will get the opportunity to have better control over their interactions and the content available on their news feeds.  It will lead to a better, more intentional, and less algorithm-based usage of social media.

    The Takeaway

    It is a fact that users cannot completely eliminate the role of Meta AI from Facebook due to the complex integration of this technology into the social media platform.  However, the above-mentioned steps are approved by experts and help to reduce the prominent influence of this feature.  Due to the cutting-edge technologies, the privacy of users has become a concern.  For this reason, it is better for Facebook users to stay updated on their privacy settings.

    By having better control over their ad preferences and content, individuals can experience a more personalized experience that is free from AI. Always make sure to stay updated about the latest Facebook policies and adjust your settings as per your choices. Try these changes and experience better Facebook usage now.
